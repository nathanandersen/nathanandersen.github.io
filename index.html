About

I am a Williams College '18 Computer Science double major. I enjoy logic and problem solving, and have an ever-growing passion for algorithms. I am considering a double major in Math. To satiate my desire for problem solving, I pursue Project Euler, in Python. I have currently solved 76 problems.

In my free time, I develop preferentially for iOS and web. I have work experience in the .NET framework and in MeteorJS (paired with ReactJS). I also have data analytics experience in Tableau and R.
